Donald J. Trump

The 45th & 47th President of the United States

Biography

Donald John Trump, born on June 14, 1946, in Queens, New York, is an American politician, media personality, and businessman who served as the 45th President of the United States from 2017 to 2021 and was elected as the 47th President in 2024, taking office in 2025.

Trump's early career was in real estate, where he expanded his father's company into a multimillion-dollar enterprise. He became a prominent figure in New York City through high-profile projects like Trump Tower.

Key Achievements

  • Launched the Space Force as the sixth branch of the U.S. Armed Forces.
  • Negotiated the Abraham Accords, fostering peace in the Middle East.
  • Passed the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, reducing corporate tax rates.
  • Operation Warp Speed accelerated COVID-19 vaccine development.
  • Appointed three Supreme Court justices during his first term.

Vision for America

In his second term, President Trump focuses on America First policies, including border security, economic growth, and energy independence.

Key initiatives include reducing regulations, promoting fair trade, and protecting American workers.

Inspiring Quotes

  • "The future belongs to the bold."
  • "Together, we will make America wealthy again."
  • "America will never be a socialist country."
